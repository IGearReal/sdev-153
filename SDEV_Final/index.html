About: LetsTalkGames

LetsTalkGames is an opinion based video game review blog, where we try out games and give you an honest and totally biased opinion of them. Here at LetsTalkGames we love a large variety of games from fast paced shooters to methodical tactical shooters, and then all the way to story and RPGs. We hope to give you a great overview of each game while also breaking down the games strengths and weaknesses.
